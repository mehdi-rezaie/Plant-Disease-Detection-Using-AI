About

Welcome to our plant disease detection website! We are dedicated to assisting users in identifying and managing plant diseases for optimal plant health and productivity. With our advanced technology, including the implementation of the CNN algorithm, we provide accurate and efficient disease detection. By uploading plant images, users can quickly receive prompt feedback and recommendations. Our user-friendly interface ensures easy navigation and access to educational resources, including articles, tutorials, and guides on plant care and sustainable farming practices. We are committed to empowering users with the knowledge and tools they need to make informed decisions about their plants.
